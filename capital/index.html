Capital Partnerships

We co-fund selected procurement cycles. Investors participate in realized profit at the deal level; timing is tied to delivery and acceptance. Principal is at risk.

How it works

We identify high-probability procurement opportunities through our AI-driven forecasting and market analysis, focusing on deals with clear fulfillment paths and established supplier relationships.

Each opportunity undergoes rigorous due diligence including cost modeling, risk assessment, and compliance verification before presenting investment terms to qualified partners.

Capital is deployed at key milestones throughout the procurement cycle, with real-time tracking of progress, costs, and compliance status to ensure on-time delivery.

Upon successful delivery and acceptance, profits are calculated and distributed according to agreed terms, with full transparency on costs, margins, and performance metrics.

What you fund

Capital is used for materials procurement, testing and certification, packaging and labeling, freight and logistics, and other direct costs associated with fulfilling defense contracts. Investors participate in profit-share on realized net profit, with returns tied to successful delivery and acceptance of goods or services. All investments are structured as profit-sharing arrangements rather than fixed returns, ensuring alignment with project outcomes.

Safeguards & Risks

Safeguards

Capital is released in milestone-based tranches tied to verified progress, with comprehensive document controls and partner confirmations at each stage. All transactions are tracked through our integrated platform, providing real-time visibility into project status, costs, and compliance requirements. Independent verification of deliverables and acceptance criteria ensures capital is only deployed when milestones are met.

Key Risks

Principal is at risk and may be lost entirely. Contract awards may be delayed, modified, or cancelled by government agencies. Supply chain disruptions, regulatory changes, or compliance issues may impact project timelines and costs. Market conditions and competitive factors may affect pricing and margins. Past performance does not guarantee future results, and there are no assurances of profit or return of capital.

Eligibility & Process

Accredited investors only. The process begins with an introductory call to discuss investment criteria and risk tolerance, followed by execution of a non-disclosure agreement and access to our data room for detailed due diligence. Investment mandates are provided on a per-deal basis, allowing investors to evaluate each opportunity individually before committing capital.
