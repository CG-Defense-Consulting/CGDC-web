Investing in Opportunities

One-time, opportunity-based investment in specific procurement programs. Each opportunity is a standalone deal—fund the program, see it through to completion, and exit. No ongoing commitment. Your capital is deployed against verified production milestones with clear guardrails and full transparency.

How it works

  • Intake & fit: Initial assessment of program requirements and funding needs
  • Structure & controls: Define funding structure, terms, and risk management protocols
  • Milestone-based draws: Capital released upon verified completion of production milestones
  • Monitoring & delivery: Continuous oversight and preparation for final delivery
  • Distribution & reporting: Final distribution and comprehensive program completion reporting

Guardrails

  • Use-of-proceeds verification and compliance tracking
  • Milestone verification before capital release
  • Escrow and approval gates for major expenditures
  • Complete audit trail for all transactions
