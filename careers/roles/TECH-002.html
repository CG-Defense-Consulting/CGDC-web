About the role

Join our AI team as a Machine Learning Engineer focused on pricing optimization and knowledge graph technologies. You'll develop sophisticated ML models that power our automated bid pricing system and build ontology services that enable intelligent procurement decision-making. This role combines cutting-edge machine learning with practical applications in defense procurement.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and deploy ML models for automated bid pricing and cost estimation
  • Build and maintain knowledge graphs and ontology services for procurement data
  • Implement graph-based algorithms for supplier relationship mapping and analysis
  • Design and optimize data pipelines for real-time ML inference
  • Collaborate with product teams to integrate ML capabilities into our platforms
  • Research and prototype new ML techniques for procurement optimization
  • Monitor model performance and implement continuous improvement processes
  • Work with large-scale datasets from federal procurement systems

Qualifications

Required

  • Master's degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field (or equivalent experience)
  • 3+ years experience in machine learning and data science
  • Strong programming skills in Python and experience with ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn)
  • Experience with graph databases and knowledge representation (Neo4j, RDF, OWL)
  • Proficiency in data processing tools (Pandas, NumPy, Spark)
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and ML deployment
  • Strong understanding of statistical modeling and optimization techniques

Nice to have

  • PhD in Machine Learning, AI, or related field
  • Experience with pricing optimization and recommendation systems
  • Knowledge of defense procurement processes and federal contracting
  • Experience with graph neural networks and knowledge graph embeddings
  • Familiarity with MLOps practices and model monitoring
  • Previous work with large-scale distributed systems
  • Publications or contributions to open-source ML projects

What we offer

  • Competitive compensation aligned with impact
  • Hybrid work flexibility
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Comprehensive health benefits
  • 401(k) with company matching
