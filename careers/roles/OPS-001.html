About the role

As an Operations Analyst at CG Group, you'll be at the heart of our defense supply chain operations, ensuring smooth procurement processes and maintaining the highest standards of quality and compliance. You'll work directly with defense contractors, government agencies, and our internal teams to optimize operations and drive efficiency across our procurement workflows.

Responsibilities

  • Manage day-to-day procurement operations and vendor coordination
  • Implement and maintain quality control workflows for defense supply chain
  • Coordinate with DLA (Defense Logistics Agency) and other federal procurement entities
  • Analyze procurement data to identify optimization opportunities
  • Ensure compliance with federal regulations and company policies
  • Support contract management and vendor relationship activities
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to streamline operations

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, or related field
  • 2+ years experience in operations, procurement, or supply chain management
  • Familiarity with federal procurement processes and regulations
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and coordination abilities
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite and data analysis tools

Nice to have

  • Experience with defense contracting or government procurement
  • Knowledge of ITAR, DFARS, and other defense compliance requirements
  • Certification in supply chain management (CPSM, CSCP, etc.)
  • Experience with procurement software and ERP systems
  • Previous work with DLA or other federal agencies

What we offer

  • Competitive compensation aligned with impact
  • Hybrid work flexibility
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Comprehensive health benefits
  • 401(k) with company matching
